## Summary

Wire Gauge & AWG Calculator is a reference utility. It does **not** collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. The app works entirely offline after installation.

---

## Data we collect

### Data we do NOT collect
- Name, email address, or any account information (no sign-in required)
- Location data
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Calculation inputs or results

### Data stored locally on your device
- Your saved calculations (stored only on your device via Android/iOS system storage)
- Your unit preference (feet vs. metres)

This data never leaves your device. We cannot access it.

### Data collected by third parties

**RevenueCat** (in-app purchase processing)  
If you purchase the Pro upgrade, RevenueCat processes the transaction on our behalf. RevenueCat may collect:
- A pseudonymous app user ID (randomly generated, not tied to your identity)
- Purchase receipt data (provided by the App Store / Google Play)
- Device platform (iOS or Android)

RevenueCat does not collect your name, email, or payment card details — those are handled entirely by Apple or Google. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

**Apple App Store / Google Play**  
Apple and Google process your payment and may collect data per their own privacy policies when you make an in-app purchase.

---

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. It contains no directed advertising and no social features.
